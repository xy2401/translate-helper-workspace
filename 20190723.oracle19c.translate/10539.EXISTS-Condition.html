条件类型 手术 例 
EXISTS

如果子查询返回至少一行，则为TRUE 。 

SELECT department_id FROM departments d WHERE EXISTS（SELECT * FROM employees e WHERE d.department_id = e.department_id）ORDER BY department_id;
