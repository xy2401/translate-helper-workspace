6.26 DBA_SYNC_CAPTURE_PREPARED_TABS

DBA_SYNC_CAPTURE_PREPARED_TABS displays information about all tables in the database that are prepared for synchronous capture instantiation. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_SYNC_CAPTURE_PREPARED_TABS.
