6.266 USER_ADDM_TASK_DIRECTIVES

USER_ADDM_TASK_DIRECTIVES displays information about ADDM task directives owned by the current user. Its columns (except for USERNAME and SEQ_ID) are the same as those in DBA_ADDM_TASK_DIRECTIVES.
