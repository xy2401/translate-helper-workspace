4.59 DBA_ANALYTIC_VIEW_LEVELS

DBA_ANALYTIC_VIEW_LEVELS describes the levels of the hierarchies of the in all of the analytic views in the database.

Its columns are the same as those in ALL_ANALYTIC_VIEW_LEVELS.
