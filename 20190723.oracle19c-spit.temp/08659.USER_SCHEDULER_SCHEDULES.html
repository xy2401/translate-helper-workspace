6.601 USER_SCHEDULER_SCHEDULES

USER_SCHEDULER_SCHEDULES displays information about the Scheduler schedules owned by the current user. Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_SCHEDULER_SCHEDULES.
