The y-axis values are the average active sessions. The x-axis values are times of day in 20 second intervals from 2:45:30 p.m. to 2:47:30 p.m.

The legend to the right of the chart has the following items: Total Activity, CPU Cores, sql_id: 75g0d6c4dh7r8, sql_id: ctv199sqz0wdg, sql_id: 14gfqcy9qw856.
