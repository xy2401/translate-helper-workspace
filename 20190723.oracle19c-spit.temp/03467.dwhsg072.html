Description of the illustration dwhsg072.eps

This figure illustrates a typical rollup for a dimension hierarchy. In it:

  • region: is at the top of the dimension hierarchy

  • subregion: is below region

  • country: is below subregion

  • state: is below country

  • city: is below state

  • customer: is at the bottom of the dimension hierarchy
