5.340 DBA_SCHEDULER_RUNNING_JOBS

DBA_SCHEDULER_RUNNING_JOBS displays information about all running Scheduler jobs in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_SCHEDULER_RUNNING_JOBS.
