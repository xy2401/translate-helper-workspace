6.702 USER_ZONEMAP_MEASURES

USER_ZONEMAP_MEASURES describes the measures for all the zone maps owned by the user. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_ZONEMAP_MEASURES.

Note:

This view is intended for use with Oracle Exadata release 12.1.2.1.1 or later.
