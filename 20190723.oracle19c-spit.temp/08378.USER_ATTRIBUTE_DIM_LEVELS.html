6.321 USER_ATTRIBUTE_DIM_LEVELS

USER_ATTRIBUTE_DIM_LEVELS describes the levels of the attribute dimensions in the current user's schema.

Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_ATTRIBUTE_DIM_LEVELS.
