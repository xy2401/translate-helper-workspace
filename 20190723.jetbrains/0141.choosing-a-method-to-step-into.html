Choosing a Method to Step Into

When you reach a line with calls of several methods, you can choose the method you want to step into.

If you choose a method of a class stepping into which is suppressed on the Stepping page of the Settings dialog box, the suppression is overridden as when you invoke the Force Step Into command.

To choose a method to step into

  1. On the main menu, choose Run | Smart Step Into or press Shift+F7.

  2. In the popup, choose the desired method from the list.
