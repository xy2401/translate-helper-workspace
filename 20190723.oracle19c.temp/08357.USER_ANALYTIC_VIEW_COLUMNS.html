6.299 USER_ANALYTIC_VIEW_COLUMNS

USER_ANALYTIC_VIEW_COLUMNS describes the columns of the analytic views owned by the current user.

Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_ANALYTIC_VIEW_COLUMNS.
