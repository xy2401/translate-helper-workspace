6.340 USER_CLUSTERING_DIMENSIONS

USER_CLUSTERING_DIMENSIONS describes dimension tables associated with tables with an attribute clustering clause owned by the user. Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_CLUSTERING_DIMENSIONS.
