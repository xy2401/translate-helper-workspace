6.662 USER_TRIGGER_ORDERING

USER_TRIGGER_ORDERING describes the triggers owned by the current user that have FOLLOWS or PRECEDES ordering. Its columns (except for TRIGGER_OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_TRIGGER_ORDERING.
