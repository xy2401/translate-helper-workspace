6.364 USER_CUBE_BUILD_PROCESSES

USER_CUBE_BUILD_PROCESSES describes the OLAP build processes and maintenance scripts owned by the current user. Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_CUBE_BUILD_PROCESSES.
