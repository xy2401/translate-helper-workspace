 AUT_DEFAULT
Table 4.3. ACTIVATION_METHOD

  • Use this parameter to specify how you want the AUT to be activated.

  • You have a choice of possible values:

    • NONE - does not activate the AUT

    • AUT_DEFAULT - uses the default from the AUT configuration

    • NE - clicks in the northeast corner of the AUT window

    • NW - clicks in the northwest corner of the AUT window

    • SE - clicks in the southeast corner of the AUT window

    • SW - clicks in the southwest corner of the AUT window

    • CENTER - clicks in the center of the AUT window

    • TITLEBAR - clicks in the titlebar. This does not work with small or non-existing titlebars
