5.2.4. The workspace perspective

In the workspace perspective (Figure 5.4, “Workspace Perspective”), you can view the Projects and files in your workspace. The workspace perspective contains the following:

  • Navigation View

  • An editor area to view e.g. Excel and HTML files.
