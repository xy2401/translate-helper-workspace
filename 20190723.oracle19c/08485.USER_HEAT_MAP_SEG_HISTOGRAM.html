6.426 USER_HEAT_MAP_SEG_HISTOGRAM

USER_HEAT_MAP_SEG_HISTOGRAM displays segment access information for segments owned by the user. Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_HEAT_MAP_SEG_HISTOGRAM.
