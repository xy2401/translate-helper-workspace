6.703 USER_ZONEMAPS

USER_ZONEMAPS describes the zone maps owned by the user. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_ZONEMAPS.

Note:

This view is intended for use with Oracle Exadata release 12.1.2.1.1 or later.
