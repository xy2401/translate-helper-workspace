6.620 USER_SQLTUNE_RATIONALE_PLAN

USER_SQLTUNE_RATIONALE_PLAN displays the association between rationales and operations in the execution plan of the SQL statements owned by the current user. Its columns are the same as those in DBA_SQLTUNE_RATIONALE_PLAN.
