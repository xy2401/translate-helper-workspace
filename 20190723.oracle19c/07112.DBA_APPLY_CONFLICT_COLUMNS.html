4.72 DBA_APPLY_CONFLICT_COLUMNS

DBA_APPLY_CONFLICT_COLUMNS displays information about the conflict handlers on all tables in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_APPLY_CONFLICT_COLUMNS.
