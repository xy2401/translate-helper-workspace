6.425 USER_GOLDENGATE_PRIVILEGES

USER_GOLDENGATE_PRIVILEGES displays details about Oracle GoldenGate privileges. Its columns (except for USERNAME) are the same as those in ALL_GOLDENGATE_PRIVILEGES.
