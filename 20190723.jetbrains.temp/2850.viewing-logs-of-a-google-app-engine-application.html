Viewing Logs of a Google App Engine Application

This feature is only supported in the Professional edition. This edition is commercial, and it provides an outstanding set of features: see the comparison matrix for more details.

To download logs of a Google App Engine

  1. On the Tools menu, choose Google App Engine, and choose Get App Engine logs.

  2. In the Get App Engine logs dialog, specify the severity of messages, and the desired period.
