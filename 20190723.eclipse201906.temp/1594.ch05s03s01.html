5.3.1. Introduction to browsers

There are three browsers in the ITE:

The Test Case Browser

lets you create and manage your Test Cases.

The Test Suite Browser

lets you create and manage your Test Suites.

The Component Names Browser

lets you see, merge add and delete component names.
