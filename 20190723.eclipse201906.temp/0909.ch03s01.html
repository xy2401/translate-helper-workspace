3.1. Requirements

To create your own ITE toolkit and remote control extensions, you need:

  • Jubula 7.0 (2.0) or later

  • Eclipse 3.4 or later (4.4 recommended)

  • JDK 5.0

  • Full write access in installation directory for deploying the extension.
