3.29.6. Editor preferences

In the Test - Editors preferences, you can alter your preferences for:

Usage counter for Component Names:

If you deactivate this option, you hide the usage counter for Component Names in the browser.

Position to add:

Here you can choose whether you want items (Test Cases and Test Steps) to be placed at the bottom of the tree branch or after the selected item when you use the add option.

Save reminder:

Here you can configure if you want to be reminded after a specified interval, if unsaved editors exist in your workspace.
