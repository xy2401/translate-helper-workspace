3.29.7. Browser preferences

In the Test - Browsers preferences, you can alter your preferences for:

Filter coloring

Here you can configure if you want the background of a browser to be colored when the filter is active. This helps to remind you that a filter is active and not all components are shown. The used background color can be changed as well.
