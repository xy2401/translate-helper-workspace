5.1.4.3.6. Check Existence in Tab Components

Check Existence of Tab

  • Use this action to check whether a tab in a tabbed pane exists.

  • The tab can be checked based on its title or its index.

  • Parameters:

    • Title

    • Operator

    • Exists

    • Timeout in Millisecs
