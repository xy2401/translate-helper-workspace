4.1.1. Setting up your Workspace

Although you can use the IDE of your choice to develop your accessibility plug-in, this guide will use Eclipse with PDE plug-ins.

  1. Once you have created your new workspace, change that workspace’s Target Platform by using the description mentioned in this manual, as well as all plug-ins that make up your AUT.

  2. Create a new Plug-in Project. The default values proposed by the wizard are acceptable, so simply enter a name for your new accessibility project and complete the wizard without additional modifications.

  3. You now have a workspace with a properly configured target platform as well as a new plug-in project. You can now begin developing your GEF toolkit extension.
