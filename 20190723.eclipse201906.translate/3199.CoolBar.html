CoolItem-Handle

This is the place at the beginning of the cool item, which is used for draging the cool item.

Available CSS Properties

Properties that can be defined for this element:

  • border

    The border of cool item handle.

  • border-bottom

    The bottom side border of cool item handle.

  • border-left

    The left side border of cool item handle.

  • border-right

    The right side border of cool item handle.

  • border-top

    The top side border of cool item handle.

  • width

    Width of cool item handle.

Applicable SWT Styles

none

Applicable States

none
